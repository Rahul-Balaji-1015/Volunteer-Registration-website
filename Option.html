What Type of Volunteer Are You ?


I Need Help





Active Volunteers are the class of "on-site" volunteers i.e., those who are prepared to provide personal-cum-material support; such may include provisions such as food and medicine or facilities such as communication and shelter. If selected, do choose your preferntial skill-of-choice from the dropdown list. Passive Volunteers are the class of "off-site" volunteers i.e., those who are prepared to offer only financial support. If selected, please identify your preferred choice of online fund transfer.
